How to use Family Ages

Adding Members

Click Add Member to create a new person. You can enter just their birth/death year, or you can provide exact months and days for perfectly precise age calculations.

Range Selection

The table displays a specific span of years. You can change this range by typing in new Start and End years, or by using the preset dropdowns next to them to quickly jump to important times (like the First Born Year).

Centering the View

If you want to focus on a specific year, use the Center tool. Pick a year from the dropdown or type it in. The table will center on that year, displaying the number of years before and after it defined by the ± box.

Year Coloring

The table cells are dynamically colored based on the person's exact age in that specific year, mapping to life stages:

  • Child (0-12)
  • Teenager (13-19)
  • Young Adult (20-39)
  • Middle Age (40-64)
  • Senior (65+)

Data Management

Use the buttons in the top right to Export your family data to a file, Import an existing file, load completely made-up Example Data () to test the app, or entirely Clear the current data.
